Explore Times Square

It wasn't until 9pm that Times Square truly became lively. People who come here seem to have forgotten time and are unwilling to go home. Cheap floats, modern shops, and I am also one of them. I remember on the evening of October 25th, my friends and I were shopping at Times Square. We had delicious food and even bought milk tea. At that moment, I was the happiest person in the world.
